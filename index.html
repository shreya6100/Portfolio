WHO AM I?

Hi, I’m Shreya—a naturally curious mind with a passion rooted in accounts, business, mathematics, and statistics. From an early age, solving equations and analyzing patterns gave me a sense of clarity and calm. As I watched the world of information technology evolve, I became fascinated by its impact and saw a powerful opportunity to combine all my interests into one dynamic field.

That’s when I discovered data analytics—a perfect intersection of logic, strategy, and technology. I chose to pursue this path, where my love for numbers meets real-world decision-making. As a lifelong learner, I’m driven by curiosity and a desire to uncover insights that make a difference. Whether it's through business analysis or storytelling with data, I’m passionate about turning information into impactful solutions.
